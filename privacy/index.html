Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 23, 2025

Genify AI ("we," "our," "us") respects your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

While using our service, we may collect the following types of information:

  • **Prompt Text:** The text you enter to generate a video. This information is sent directly to our AI model to create your video.
  • **Technical Information:** When you visit our website, our server automatically records certain technical information, such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, and the time of your visit.
  • **Cookies:** We may use cookies to improve our website and personalize your experience.

2. How We Use Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • **To Provide the Service:** To generate videos using the prompts you provide.
  • **To Improve the Service:** We may use anonymous data to improve the performance of our AI model and website.
  • **For Security:** To secure our website and services and to prevent abuse.
  • **For Analytics:** We may use technical information to understand website traffic and user behavior.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with any third parties, except in the following cases:

  • **Service Providers:** We may use third-party service providers, such as a video generation API, to operate our services. These providers are only authorized to use your information for the purposes for which we have hired them.
  • **Legal Requirements:** We may disclose your information if we are legally required to do so.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of data transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • The right to access your data.
  • The right to correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • The right to have your data erased ("right to be forgotten").

To exercise these rights, please contact us.

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post any changes on this page.
